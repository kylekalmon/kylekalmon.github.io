About Us

We are local Tipton County residents that want to keep our home beautiful by lovingly restoring those properties that need a little TLC. We want Tipton County to remain the beautiful safe area we grew up in so our children can enjoy everything the area has to offer.

We are always on the look out for properties in need of some TLC. We can't be everywhere which is why we need tips from neighbors to identify these properties that need to be restored. Condition of the property does not matter. We are not afraid to get our hands dirty. We are able to close on properties within 30, sometimes faster and our offers are always cash.

The Process

  • Step: 1

    Contact us and tell us more about the property you want us to rehab. Include things like the address, condition, your contact information and why you want to sell or why you think it needs to be fixed up.

  • Step: 2

    We will do some online research of the house. Things like the neighborhood its in, Google street and sattelite view.

  • Step: 3

    If we like what we see so far we will reach out to you to schedule a time to look at the property. We generally respond within 24 hours.

  • Step: 4

    If everything to this point looks good, we will make an offer and start negotiations with the owners of the propery.

  • Step: 5

    If our offer is accepted we can generally close within 30 days with no realtor fees.
